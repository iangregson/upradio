Welcome to UpRadio!

UpRadio is a tool you can use to broadcast audio to anyone in the world or listen to a broadcast from anyone in the world.

Please do not broadcast content that you do not own the copyright for or do not otherwise have permission to broadcast. Please use this tool for good and don't do bad things.

Privacy

You may have noticed the lack of a pop up about cookies. That's because this web application doesn't use them. It doesn't care about your identity and doesn't want to serve advertisements.

Isn't that refreshing.

Some information you enter ento the application (like channel name, description, etc) is stored locally in the browser's session and local storage caches. It is shared with other listeners peer-to-peer and is not transferred via centralized servers. Same goes for your audio content.
